About Shanshan Zheng

Ms. Zheng brings to you a combined six years of experiences in litigation, immigration, business and family law. She is trial lawyer particularly experienced in employment litigation, deportation defense, as well as criminal and family matters. With the breadth and depth of her practices, Ms. Zheng brings to her clients well-rounded services and in-depth knowledge of the legal industry.

Indeed, the law is not just the words in the books; it is a way to bringing practical solutions to businesses and individuals in a cost-effective manner. With her commitment to excellence, ethics and grace, Ms. Zheng strives to be a different kind of lawyer who offers the best communication, the most honest and meticulously researched legal advice, as well as a sophisticated, goal-oriented legal strategy - all stemming from her genuine care for her clients.

Ms. Zheng obtained her Juris Doctor degree from The George Washington University Law School and is a member of the New York Bar. She is fluent in English and Mandarin Chinese, and speaks conversational German.
